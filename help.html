Frequently Asked Questions

8 articles

Quick answers to common banking questions.

Go to Transfers in the sidebar. Select your source account, enter the destination account number or choose from your saved payees, enter the amount, and confirm. Transfers between your own NexusBank accounts are instant. External transfers may take 1–3 business days.

Open Transactions, find the charge you want to dispute, and tap Report a problem. Fill in the dispute form and submit. Our team will investigate within 5–10 business days and email you with the outcome. For urgent fraud, use the Contact Us page to reach us immediately.

Go to Security in the sidebar and toggle on Two-factor authentication. You will be prompted to verify your email address. Once confirmed, a one-time code will be required every time you sign in from a new device.

Standard accounts have a daily outbound transfer limit of $10,000. Premium accounts have a limit of $50,000. Limits reset at midnight UTC. To request a temporary increase, submit a support ticket below or contact us directly.

Visit your Profile page from the sidebar. You can update your display name, phone number, and address there. Changes to your legal name or date of birth require identity verification — submit a support ticket and our team will guide you through the process.

Go to Cards in the sidebar, select the card you want to manage, and tap the Freeze card toggle. Freezing is instant and prevents all new transactions. You can unfreeze at any time. If your card is lost or stolen, use the Report lost/stolen option to permanently block it and request a replacement.

Open Statements from the sidebar. Select the account and the date range you need, then click Download PDF. Statements are generated instantly for the past 24 months. For older records, submit a support ticket and we will prepare them within 2 business days.

On the Sign In page, click Forgot password? and enter your registered email address. You will receive a reset link within a few minutes. The link expires after 1 hour. If you do not receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.
