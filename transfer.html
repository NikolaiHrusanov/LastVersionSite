Instant Transfers

Send money anywhere in seconds, for free

What is Instant Transfers?

Instant Transfers lets you send money directly to anyone — a friend, a family member, or a business — in seconds, not days. There are no hidden fees, no waiting for clearing windows, and no paperwork. Just enter who you're sending to, the amount, and hit send. Your money arrives in real time, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including weekends and holidays.

1

Choose a Recipient

Pick from your saved contacts or enter a new bank account number, email address, or mobile number. We verify the recipient before any money moves.

2

Enter the Amount

Type in any amount using the keypad or tap a quick preset. You can also add a note — like "Rent" or "Dinner" — so both sides know what it's for.

3

Review & Confirm

Double-check all the details on the review screen — recipient, amount, and account. Our fraud detection runs automatically in the background to keep you safe.

4

Money Arrives Instantly

Once confirmed, the funds leave your account and land in the recipient's account within seconds. You both get a real-time notification when it's done.
